Meteor Math

Objective: Solve arithmetic problems to destroy incoming meteors before they hit the planet.

  • Watch the Meteors: Arithmetic problems appear on meteors falling from the top.
  • Solve the Problems: Type the correct answer into the input box at the bottom and press Enter.
  • Destroy Meteors: If your answer is correct, the corresponding meteor is destroyed.
  • Score Points: Each destroyed meteor increases your score.
  • Avoid Meteors Hitting the Planet: The game ends if any meteor reaches the bottom.

Color Collector

Objective: Collect colors to create a target color as quickly as possible.

  • Match the Target Color: Click the colored circles to mix their colors into your current color to match the target color circle.
  • Reset Anytime: Click "Reset Game" to start over with a different target color.
  • Winning Condition: You win when your mixed color is within a small range of the target color.

Echo Tracker

Objective: Listen to a sequence of sounds and repeat it using keyboard keys.

  • Listen to the Sequence: The game will play a sequence of tones. Watch and listen carefully.
  • Repeat the Sequence: Use the 'A', 'S', 'D', 'F' keys to repeat the sequence of sounds.
  • Advance Through Rounds: Each time you correctly repeat the sequence, the game adds another tone.
  • Winning: Continue through rounds until you make a mistake.

Rocket Dodge

Objective: Control a rocket to avoid incoming asteroids and survive as long as you can.

  • Gameplay: The rocket automatically moves forward through space. Asteroids come from the right side of the screen, and the player must avoid them by moving the rocket up and down using the arrow keys (or tap on the screen for mobile).
  • Difficulty: The game gets progressively harder as more asteroids appear and they move faster.
  • Scoring: The score increases the longer you survive, displayed in the top left corner.
